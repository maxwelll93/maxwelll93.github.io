Max's Calgary Adventure List

Things to Do

  • Fish Creek Park

    Enjoy a leisurely stroll or bike ride in one of North America's largest urban parks, surrounded by nature.

  • Heritage Park

    Step back in time for a charming date with historical exhibits and a recreated village ambiance.

  • Studio Bell

    Explore the vibrant music scene on your date at the National Music Centre.

Winter Activities

  • Ice Skating at Olympic Plaza

    Hold hands and glide gracefully on the ice for a magical winter date in downtown Calgary.

  • Winter Festivals

    Bundle up and explore festive winter markets and events for a cozy and joyful date.

  • Skiing at Lake Louise

    I recently took snowboarding lessons but still have not gone to the mountains. We can arm up with hot cocoa after hitting the slopes for a romantic winter wonderland date.

  • Nose Hill Park

    We can cross-country ski in Nose Hill Park.

Places to Eat

  • Charbar

    Location: Simmons Building, 618 Confluence Way SE

    Cuisine: Argentinian-inspired grill and barbecue. Charbar offers a diverse menu with a focus on locally sourced ingredients and an extensive selection of meats.

  • Model Milk

    Location: 308 17 Ave SW

    Cuisine: Modern Canadian cuisine with a focus on local and seasonal ingredients. Model Milk is known for its inventive dishes and stylish atmosphere.

  • Ten Foot Henry

    Location: 1209 1 St SW

    Cuisine: A vegetable-forward restaurant serving family-style dishes. The menu is designed for sharing and features a variety of fresh and flavorful options.

  • Anju

    Location: 344 17 Ave SW

    Cuisine: Korean-inspired small plates and shareable dishes. Anju is known for its creative take on traditional Korean flavors and its chic, modern atmosphere.

  • Pigeonhole

    Location: 306 17 Ave SW

    Cuisine: Modern European small plates with a focus on bold flavors and creative combinations. Pigeonhole is known for its unique dishes and carefully curated wine list.

  • Alforno Bakery & Café

    Location: 222 7 St SW

    Cuisine: A European-style bakery and café offering a variety of fresh-baked goods, sandwiches, and salads. Alforno is a great spot for breakfast or a casual lunch.

  • Klein/Harris

    Location: 110 8 Ave SW

    Cuisine: Contemporary Canadian cuisine with a focus on locally sourced ingredients. Klein/Harris offers a refined dining experience in a stylish setting.

  • Vero Bistro Moderne

    Location: 209 10 St NW

    Cuisine: A modern European bistro offering a mix of French and Italian-inspired dishes. Vero Bistro Moderne is known for its intimate atmosphere and seasonally changing menu.

  • Peace Bridge

    Take a stroll or bike ride across the iconic Peace Bridge with stunning views.

  • Calgary Central Library

    Get cozy and explore the architecturally stunning library for a unique and charming date.

  • Scotsman's Hill

    Experience a picturesque date with a panoramic view of downtown Calgary and the Bow River.

  • Devonian Gardens

    Escape to an indoor paradise in the heart of downtown for a serene and romantic date.

  • Nose Hill Park ✓

    Hold hands and enjoy a romantic hike or bike ride through Nose Hill Park
